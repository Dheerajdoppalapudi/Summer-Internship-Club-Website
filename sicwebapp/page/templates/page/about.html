About Us

SUMMER INTERNSHIP CLUB is a special interest group of GITAM. We are oriented in all field of study.

Things We Do

✅ Our group focuses on upcoming training opportunities (Research Intern, Dissertation projects, Jobs, etc.) and related information with the latest workshops/conferences/webinars meant for one's career.

✅ To create passion towards various opportunities that are coming from multiple fields of study

✅ Notes/ebooks are also provided

✅ Interaction with different expertise

✅ Provides exam notifications on time

We have three WhatsApp groups with 500 members approx. So far, with the help of the information provided by our club, 70 to 80 students have received internships in top universities and research labs like IASc, Canadian fellowship, IITs, and many more. Our extensive network of approachable experts helps you identify and secure the best resources for your career development.

Frequently Asked Questions ❓


Why should I join Summer Internship Club?

the main moto of SIC is learn - build - grow with the community. we help student of GITAM to find internship opportunities

We conduct a minimum of 2 events every month, in addition to competetions, the events are not constrained to any particular year of students.

The clubs is open to any student, ranging from 1st years to final years who have a zeal to help other students.

All the event news are available under the Events section of this site, one can also join whatsapp group to keep themselves updated about the same.
